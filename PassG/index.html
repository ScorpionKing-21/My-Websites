Tips about choosing password

First of all, the password should be minimum 10 characters not 8, because it is nowadays easy to crack 8 characters password with just simple bruteforce method. Secondly, your password should not be simple words like someone's name and birthday john1234. Hackers can use dictonary attack, word-list attack. your password should include upper, lower and special characters which is helpful in creating strong password.

Tips about choosing password

First of all, the password should be a minimum of 10 characters, not 8, because it is nowadays easy to crack 8 characters password with just a simple brute force method. Secondly, your password should not be simple words like someone's name and birthday john1234. Hackers can use a dictionary attack, word-list attack. Your password should include upper, lower, and special characters that help create a strong password.
